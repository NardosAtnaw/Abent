Long-term Partnership

Are you looking for a long-term partnership that will allow you to receive our services on a consistent basis? Through a retainership agreement, we are happy to provide our services to businesses on an ongoing basis. There are two options for engaging us through a retainer agreement.


Option A: A fixed monthly fee for a set period of time:
When you opt up for this service, our attorney will be available for a given amount of time each month for a monthly fixed fee. This removes the need to search for a lawyer every time you need to work on a certain legal issue or problem. It also gives you a reason to keep us involved in your business rather than waiting until a legal issue becomes too big to ignore


Additionally, by choosing the fixed monthly fee retainer service, your firm will pay a lower charge for any time we spent outside of your plan or for any additional legal work not covered by your fixed monthly fee retainer agreement

Please note that this option is only available to a limited number of firms in order to give the best possible availability and service to those clients.


Option B: A monthly retainer fee combined with a set fee for future services:
For as little as $100 per month, our office will enter into a Retainer Agreement with you, defining the terms and conditions of our future engagement whenever you require our legal services. If you do not use our services, you only pay a small fixed monthly fee to retain our office. Whenever your firm requires any of our services, you will be charged the fee and subject to the terms and conditions outlined in our Retainer Agreement.


If you select this option, you will have the assurance that whenever you require any of our services, we will provide them in accordance with the terms and conditions outlined in our Retainer Agreement. This gives you peace of mind about the cost of any future legal services you may require


Please Let Us Know if you have any other suggestions, as we are interested in exploring different options for our long-term partnership engagement.
