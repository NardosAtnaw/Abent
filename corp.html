General Business Council

For small and medium-sized businesses as well as start-ups, hiring a dedicated in-house lawyer to oversee and manage all of the legal matters they encounter is not always practical or cost-effective, unlike large corporations and businesses. As a result, many small and medium-sized businesses seek the assistance of outside legal counsel.


AbnetLaw provides the best outside general counsel services in Ethiopia for international and domestic small- and medium-sized businesses as well as start-ups on an as-needed basis


At AbnetLaw, we provide business counseling across a wide spectrum of corporate and business matters, includes, company formation & branch registration, intellectual property, mergers, acquisitions, and dispositions, labor & employment, business succession, commercial and business laws, investment and privatization, partnerships and joint ventures, taxation, import and export, shipping & maritime law, internal investigations, general corporate law and business contract matters, contract negotiation and drafting, partnership and joint ventures, regulatory compliance, licence and registration, non disclosure, non-competition & non solicitation agreements, and copyrights and trademarks.

Our general business counseling service not only provides a comprehensive and cost-effective solution for our clients, but it also comes with a variety of billing options.
