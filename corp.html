Corporate Business Council

Looking for sound legal advice on matters that arise in your business and/or legal interest in Ethiopia? AbnetLaw can serve you as a pillar of legal stability to help you manage compliance requirements, risk management decisions, and provide guidance on all your business undertakings in Ethiopia.


We advise domestic and international clients on a wide range of issues, including foreign investment & privatizations, mergers & acquisitions, business & company formation, partnerships & joint ventures, purchases & sales of businesses, taxation, import & export, employment, corporate reorganization & restructuring, and general corporate as well as corporate governance issues.


We also draft legal documents and review existing ones to make sure they protect our clients’ rights as well as to ensure they are enforceable under Ethiopian legal jurisdiction. Furthermore, we provide full assistance to our clients on a variety of licenses and certificates for their business. We also prepare and follow up on our clients’ legal requests and applications to any Addis Ababa and federal government agencies and offices.
