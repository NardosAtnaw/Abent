Representation in Litigations and Tribunals

AbnetLaw represents both international and domestic clients who seek to protect and enforce their legal rights in Ethiopia. We prepare cases for court adjudication and represent our clients as advocates as fervently as necessary in all aspects of complex business and commercial litigation, including arbitral tribunals, settlement venues, and forums.

Our Law Office has been engaged in litigation since the very beginning of its activities; and after years of successfully presenting clients in numerous court cases, we can affirm that we are the leading law office in handling civil and commercial court cases in Ethiopia.

We handle a wide range of business litigation matters, including Breach of Contract, Business Dissolution or Breakup Disputes, Copyright and Trademark Litigation, Fraud and Misrepresentation, Misappropriation of Trade Secrets, Tax Appeals and Assessment Challenges, Partnership and Shareholder Disputes, Real State & Construction Litigation, and Tort.

Our litigation practice also includes representing clients at arbitration, administrative and judicial hearings.
