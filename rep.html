Representation & Business Litigation

AbnetLaw represents both international and domestic businesses in Ethiopia who seek to protect and enforce their legal rights. AbnetLaw prepares cases for court adjudication and advocates for our clients in all aspects of complex business, commercial, and civil litigation, including arbitral tribunals, settlement venues, and forums.


At AbnetLaw, we regularly represent clients in all levels of federal and Addis Ababa municipality courts, as well as in alternative dispute resolution forums such as mediation and arbitration.


Our litigation and representation practice includes a wide variety of commercial and business disputes, including fraud, contract disputes, unfair competition and business practices, intellectual property, trade secrets, patent, trademark and copyright litigation, breach of warranties, labor and employment dispute, injunctions, insurance dispute, and liability and tort claims


Our attorney has an extensive experience in representing clients before federal and Addis Ababa government agencies and authorities. Furthermore, he assists our clients in obtaining a variety of licenses, permits and certificates for their businesses.
