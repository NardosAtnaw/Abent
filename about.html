Abnet Solomon Law Office (aka AbnetLaw) is an Addis Ababa-based legal services provider we were founded with the inspiration to elevate the legal services experience in Ethiopia to a higher level of efficiency, and with the clear goal of becoming a client-focused, customized, and individualized leading legal services provider in Ethiopia defined through a reputation of consistent professionalism and diligence.

At AbnetLaw our philosophy is guided by representing fewer clients at a time. We believe that this mode of operation allows us to provide more intimate attention to each of our clients and their cases, we consider our firm to be client-centric and a best-in-class legal services provider in Ethiopia. We assure our clients that AbnetLaw is uniquely positioned to personalize the client experience by means of offering both dedicated and personalized attention to each client and their respective case. We appreciate that each case/legal problem is uniquely personal for our client, with specific economic, social, and emotional interests intrinsically attached to their respective bestowed interests. In other words, we believe that seemingly identical cases or legal problems cannot always be solved by applying the all-out same legal recourse. Therefore, we always exert intricate efforts to thoroughly and exclusively evaluate and render individualized attention/bespoke solutions to each case/legal problem we undertake, on its merit. We process each case with sensitivity and equal passion.

We provide a wide variety of legal, para-legal, and quasi-legal services to multinational business firms, governmental and non-governmental organizations, as well as sole individuals. Our services are accessible remotely or in person, in office, or as per your choice depending on the nature of the services you require. We are committed to delivering you a client-centric, robust and comprehensive legal service with the highest possible level of reliability, integrity, and professionalism.
