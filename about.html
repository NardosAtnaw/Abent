Abnet Solomon Law Office (aka AbnetLaw) is a legal services provider based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. The inspiration behind the incorporation of AbnetLaw is to elevate the experience of legal services in Ethiopia by providing efficient, client-focused, and bespoke legal services.

At AbnetLaw, the philosophy is to represent the clients effectively in a timely fashion defined through a reputation of consistent professionalism and diligence. We believe that this mode of operation allows us to provide more personalized attention to each of our clients and their respective cases. As we are a client-centric firm, therefore, our clients always have the assurance that AbnetLaw will always be uniquely positioning them as per their personalized clientele needs by offering both dedicated and personalized attention and respective services on a case by case basis We appreciate that each case/legal problem is uniquely personal for our client, with specific economic, social, and emotional interests intrinsically attached to their respective bestowed interests. In other words, we believe that seemingly identical cases or legal problems cannot always be solved by applying the same legal recourse. Therefore, we always exert intricate efforts to thoroughly and exclusively evaluate and render individualized attention/bespoke solutions to each case/legal problem that we undertake. Thus, we process each case with sensitivity and equal passion.

We provide a wide variety of legal, para-legal, and quasi-legal services to multinational business firms, governmental and non-governmental organizations, as well as sole individuals. Our services are accessible remotely or in person, in the office, or as per your choice depending on the nature of the services you require. We are committed to delivering you a client-centric, robust, and comprehensive legal service with the highest possible level of reliability, integrity, and professionalism
