Legal Due Diligence

Legal due diligence is a required component of any merger or acquisition transaction, as well as any initial investment in a foreign country with diverse investment and commercial laws. AbnetLaw provides multinational small- and medium-sized businesses and entrepreneurs in Ethiopia with bestin-class, tailored legal due diligence services.


Our due diligence services are focused on providing critical and adequate information after collecting all available information on an individual, company, or business transaction in question, in order to help our clients understand the risks associated with their investment and the cost-effectiveness of a business transaction


The primary goal of our due diligence service is to identify and assess the strengths and weaknesses of a business transaction, as well as to evaluate the legal risks and benefits of the transaction and the best method to mitigate any potential or unexpected risks
