About Us

Abnet Solomon Law Office (aka AbnetLaw) is an Addis Ababa-based legal services provider we were founded with the inspiration to elevate the legal services experience in Ethiopia to a higher level of efficiency, and with the clear goal of becoming a client-focused, customized, and individualized leading legal services provider in Ethiopia defined through a reputation of consistent professionalism and diligence.

At AbnetLaw our philosophy is guided by representing fewer clients at a time. We believe that this mode of operation allows us to provide more intimate attention to each of our clients and their cases, we consider our firm to be client-centric and a best-in-class legal services provider in Ethiopia. We assure our clients that AbnetLaw is uniquely positioned to personalize the client experience by means of offering both dedicated and personalized attention to each client and their respective case.

Meet Our Attorney

Abnet S. Melkamu is the founder and owner of the Law Office of Abnet Solomon (aka AbnetLaw). He is admitted to practice law in all levels of the Federal Courts. He is also admitted to practice before the Addis Ababa city municipality courts. His practices encompass all kinds of business transactions and business disputes.

Being an attorney has been a long-standing goal for Abnet, and he founded AbnetLaw in 2018 with the ambition of improving the quality of Ethiopian legal services by introducing a tailor-made, unique legal service and providing all-round, one-stop, multi-level, high-quality services for the best satisfaction of his clients. Since then, his main focus has been continuing to provide customized and personalized legal services to international medium and small businesses.
